About Us

Since 2007, FACES is Oman’s premier leisure magazine, for discerning readers with distinguished tastes. Through exceptional content and visual appeal, FACES highlights the best in local and international lifestyle trends, fashion, cuisine, travel, health and fitness and much more.


A full-colour, glossy monthly publication, FACES’ USP is that it constantly profiles Omani personalities of note or interest from diverse fields as its Cover Story, that has developed a strong link with its readers.


With the new look and format, we expand our editorial coverage with exciting new features:


  • Latest launches & happenings in town
  • Prominent Omani personalities – young achievers
  • Face of the Month
  • Driving
  • Gourmet
  • Home & Health
  • Connoisseur/Luxury
  • Fashion/Beauty
  • Shopping
  • Men’s features & sections
  • Finance
  • Glitz & Glamour
  • Local News
  • Searching FACES directory – what to do, where to eat

Distributed FREE with the leading national day Times of Oman, more than 20,000 copies are spread out between the newspapers’ subscribers, restaurants, hotels, coffee outlets, clinics, health clubs, corporate offices, government and private organisations, ministries, art galleries, travel agencies and many more.
